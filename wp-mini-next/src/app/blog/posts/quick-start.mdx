## About

WattDownload is the best and fastest available Story downloader for WP. To say how fast, you can download most stories under 5 seconds and almost all stories under 10 seconds.

<CodeBlock compact marginBottom="16" codes={[

<CodeBlock compact marginBottom="16" codes={[
  {
    code: "npm install",

  }
]} />

<CodeBlock compact marginBottom="16" codes={[
  {

  }
